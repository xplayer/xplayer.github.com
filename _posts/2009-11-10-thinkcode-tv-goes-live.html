ThinkCode.TV is a website specializes in the delivery of high quality commercial screencasts about software development, at a really cheap prices. The first 5 videos are now online (the actual language is italian, but english content is planned for the following months):
  • The first two lessions on Python, by Marco Beri
  • A screencast on MacRuby, by Renzo Borgatti
  • The first two lessions on TDD, by Piergiuliano Bossi.
Selling all these high-quality screencasts at about 5 euro each makes them *really* appetible.
