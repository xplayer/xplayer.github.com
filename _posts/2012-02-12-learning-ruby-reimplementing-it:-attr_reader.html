How handy is the attr_reader method? Very handy indeed.

Never asked yourself how it may be implemented? Just do it!

This is my take:

module Kernel
 def attribute_reader(attribute)
   define_method(attribute) do
     instance_variable_get("@#{attribute}")
   end
 end
end
